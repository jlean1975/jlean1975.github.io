What do we do?

Modelling Team

  • Electricity Demand and Generation Scenarios (EDGS)
  • National policy statement on urban development. Used data from:
    • Tenancy bond database for rents and bonds lodged
    • CoreLogic database for sales
    • Rating valuations
    • Building characteristics
    • StatsNZ building consents data

Modelling Team

  • Quarry project
    • 600 surveys generated in R and emailed to individual quarries
    • Data received and processed in R
    • Start to finish programmatic execution
    • Much improved quality of data and outputs

Modelling Team Expertise

  • R programming
  • Shiny data visualisation tools

  • Automated document generation

Energy Team

  • Monitor oil, gas, coal, electricity, GHG emissions, and prices
  • Report on balances to International Energy Agency monthly and annually
  • Price monitoring of electricity, fuel, and fuel taxes
  • Publish quarterly and annual summaries of the energy sector
  • Collaborate with other Ministries such as EECA, MoT

Energy Team Expertise

  • Some Javascript for web-based data visualisation tools
  • Energy team currently migrating from Excel into the R environment

Show me the data!

Oil and Gas

  • Fields - Pohokura, Maui, Kapuni, McKee etc
  • Oil companies - BP, Z, Mobil, Gull
  • Refining NZ
  • Coastal Oil Logistics Limited (COLL)
  • Customs biofuel data
  • Some smaller returns such as bitumen, petcoke, LPG
  • Some data is monthly, some is quarterly
  • Data is just volumes/tonnage. We don't track dollar values.

Electricity

  • Data sourced from
    • Major generators provide data monthly by email
    • Electricity authority provides data monthly via a download from the EA website
    • Small generators provide data annually by email
  • Generation data is used in our quarterly and annual publications
  • Used to inform decision making by industry

Coal

  • Data sourced from
    • Coal mines
    • Genesis Energy
    • StatsNZ
  • Coal survey is sent out quarterly
  • Used to track reserves, production and consumption

Prices

  • Quarterly Survey of Domestic Electricity Prices (QSDEP)
  • Quarterly Retail Sales Survey (QRSS)
  • Weekly Fuel Margins

Greenhouse Gas

  • Some data is drawn from existing sources such as oil and gas reporting, or coal etc
  • This data is then used to estimate emissions volumes using various analytical methods
  • Any data gaps are then met by direct returns from emitters
